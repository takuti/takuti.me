<i class="fa fa-map-marker"></i> It's a beautiful season in Ottawa! The city is so green, flowers are blooming, and the temperature is moderate.

### Search for a new (remote) opportunity

It's been 8 months without big trips (very unusual for me, except the COVID period), and the eventless life is likely to continue for a while for personal reasons. Even though I like maintaining daily routines in quietness, the condition also brings me a subtle sense of boredom and dissatisfaction. My freelancing work is also more stable nowadays, which is good and bad. So, I'm now open to work. I genuinely miss the exposure to complex problems and the serendipitous interactions with others in customer-facing work.

### 5 months of practicing French

I've consistently spent 3+ hours per day in learning French since January, and I *think* it has been improving at a decent pace. In particular, reading online articles, writing [blog posts](/fr/note), and listening to not-too-fast podcasts (like [this](https://innerfrench.com/podcast/) and [this](https://ici.radio-canada.ca/ohdio/balados/6108/ca-sexplique-balado-info-alexis-de-lancer)) have become more effortless. Speaking, on the other hand, is still far from conversational fluency. Even though I'm working with an amazing tutor on [italki](https://www.italki.com/en/i/ref/Ae0AccD?hl=en&utm_medium=user_referral&utm_source=copylink_share) multiple times a week, I'm feeling the plateau, and I definitely need more opportunities to talk with people in the real world.

### Failed attempt for sub-1:30 half marathon

This year's [Ottawa Half Marathon](https://www.runottawa.ca/races-and-events/half-marathon/) ended, and I finished in 1:32:02, which was a little slower than [my PB](https://www.notion.so/takuti/Running-0c7b35e5463f4c1e9d368ed1c4000eba?source=copy_link#b0081501ac1a480a96037787f3868535). Although it was wet on the race day, the temperature was perfectly cool in the breeze. So, the problem was my pace and the lack of long-distance practice due to the prolonged winter. Honestly, I'm feeling sad and disappointed, but I'll take advantage of the summer weather to stay active and possibly make another attempt in [September](https://armyrun.ca).
