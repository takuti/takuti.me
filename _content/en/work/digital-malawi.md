### Scope

Led digital product and infrastructure strategy at a Malawian non-profit, from in-field user research and capacity assessment to mobile app delivery and web performance optimization. Managed a team of 10 communication and IT professionals as an Acting Director while representing the organization nationwide through conference presentations, workshop facilitation, and stakeholder engagements.

### Technology

- **Domains**: Education, Non-profit Operations, Digital Marketing
- **Platforms**: Google Analytics, HubSpot, Power BI, Tableau
- **Implementation**: Python, SQL, WordPress, JavaScript, .NET (MAUI), AWS, cPanel

### Key Contributions

- **Strategic Guidance**: Conducted a capability assessment across security, privacy, data, and AI domains, translating a 5-year organizational vision into an actionable digital roadmap with KPI tracking across communications, IT, and data priorities
- **Digital Marketing Implementation**: Led analytics-driven content and engagement strategy for the organization's WordPress web presence, [emergelivelihoods.org](https://emergelivelihoods.org), achieving 8x year-over-year traffic growth while improving accessibility and frontend performance
- **Mobile App Delivery in Low-Resource Context**: Refined UI/UX design, roadmap, and outsourcing strategy of an e-learning application, unblocking the app release in 3 months while achieving 80% cost reduction
