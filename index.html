Hi! I'm Takuya Kitazawa, aka takuti.

I'm bridging a gap between scientific theory and real-world practice in industry. At Arm Treasure Data, an organization building an enterprise-grade big data analytics platform, I have been practically acted as a data scientist, technical evangelist, consultant, machine learning engineer and software engineer through the experience of contributing to OSS for scalable ML, implementing out-of-the-box ML application, presenting at conferences, and working on a variety of customer-facing opportunities. My current interest is particularly in large-scale ML and its UI/UX matter, especially in the context of recommender systems and data streams.
