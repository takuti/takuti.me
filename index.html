Hi! I'm Takuya Kitazawa, aka takuti.

I'm working on machine learning and data science at Arm Treasure Data, an Arm's internal organization developing large-scale enterprise customer data platform. My mission is to connect theory and practice in their B2B business. So far, I have been practically acted as a data scientist, data analyst, consultant, machine learning engineer and software engineer through the implementation of scalable ML library, out-of-the-box ML application, and customer-specific solutions on the wide variety of data.

Things I like

  • Traveling. In Japan, I like countryside and small cities like my hometown, Nagano. Oslo (Norway) and Vancouver (Canada) are two of the best foreign cities I've ever visited.
  • Craft beer, especially Amber Ales, and Japanese sake. While traveling, I enjoy visiting breweries and drinking something local.
  • Burgers. Good beer and good burger, that's the combination.
